CALCATRON 500 accepts Keyboard & Numpad Inputs:

  •      "+" for Addition
  •      "-" for Subtraction
  •      "x" or "*" for Multiplication
  •      "/" for Division
  •      "." for Decimal
  •      "m" to toggle Positive/Negative sign
  •      "Enter" for Equal/Solve
  •     "Backspace" to delete last entry
  •     "ESC" for C [Clear] or CE [Clear Entry]
